About me - MH Qureshi
30+ years of experience with 100+ solved cases

M.H. Qureshi Law Associates, under the leadership of Supreme Court Advocate Muhammad Habib Qureshi, offers distinguished legal representation built upon over 29 years of extensive experience since his initial enrolment in 1994. Our firm, located at Office No. 50, 2nd Floor, Liberty Mall, University Road, Peshawar, is committed to providing sophisticated legal solutions that leverage this unique blend of deep local expertise and a global perspective for every client.
